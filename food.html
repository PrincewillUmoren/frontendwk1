Food File

There are six classes of food namely;

carbohydrate
any of a large group of organic compounds that includes sugars, starch, and cellulose

Three Examples of carbohydrate

  1. Bread
  2. Milk
  3. beans
fat and Oil
they are esters of glycerol with three fatty acids.

Three examples of fat and oil

  1. Peanut
  2. Canola oil
  3. Palm oil
Protein
A molecule made up of amino acids

Three examples of protein

  1. Egg
  2. Milk
  3. Lobsters
Vitamins
A nutrient that the body needs in small amounts to function and stay healthy

Three examples of vitamins

  1. Green leaf
  2. Citrus
  3. Strawberries
Mineral
Minerals are those elements on the earth and in foods that our bodies need to develop and function normally.

Three examples of mineral

  1. meat
  2. Cereal
  3. Fish
Water
water, a substance composed of the chemical elements hydrogen and oxygen and existing in gaseous, liquid, and solid states

Three eaxmples

  1. Lettuce
  2. broth
  3. Skim milk
